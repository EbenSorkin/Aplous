Alatsi is an original typeface designed by Spyros Zevelakis.

Alatsi is a semicondensed geometric sans design.

Alatsi feels familiar, calm, trustable and contemporary. It is a little lighthearted or casual in feeling as well. The contemporary feeling comes from the treatment of terminals and the cheekily pointed V A W. The calmness from the modest x height. Alatsi offers a wide range of figures including Oldstyle figures, small numbers including superiors and fractions. Alata also offers case sensitive forms.
